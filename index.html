Here's a timeline of Ajit Doval life

  • 1945- Born in Ghiri Banelsyun
  • 1967- Graduated with Master's Degree in Economics
  • 1968- Joined IPS in the Kerala Cadre
  • 1971-1999: Terminated 15 Hijack attempts on Indian Airlines
  • 1988-Awarded one of the highest gallantry awards, the Kirti Chakra
  • 2005-Retired as Director of Intelligence Bureau
  • 2009-Founder Director of Vivekananda Internation Foundation
  • 2014-Appointed as India's fifth National Security Adviser
  • 2014-played a crucial role in ensuring secure return of 46 Indian nurses who were trapped in Iraq
